The Couple

Kevin and Robin met in San Diego through some amazing friends at the beach. After many great adventures and homes through out San Diego, they settled down in South Park with their new puppy Koby. This past March, Kevin & Robin got engaged in Kauai on a hike overlooking the ocean. Down on one knee, Kevin finally ask Robin with a ring pasted to him by his Grammie Baker. So very special.

Kevin Baker the groom

Kevin grew up in Boxborough MA. He moved to San Diego seventeen years ago where he developed his career as an entrepreneur and software developer. Kevin enjoys cycling, art and playing with gadgets.

Robin Hill the bride

Robin grew in Missoula MT. She moved to San Diego eighteen years ago where she grew her career as mortgage consultant. Robin loves cuddling with her puppy, exercise and making yummy food with Kevin.
